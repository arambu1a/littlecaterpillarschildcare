about us

Building Bright Futures, One Step at a Time

Little Caterpillars Childcare has helped children build strong educational and social foundations - all while embracing the joy of childhood.

Our programs focus on school readiness, emotional and intellectual growth, literacy, problem-solving, and providing the love and care every child needs.

From infants to school-age children we offer nationally-recognized curriculum designed to prepare your child for a lifeimte of learning.
